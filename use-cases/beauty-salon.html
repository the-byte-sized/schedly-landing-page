Why Schedly for Beauty Salons?

Transform your salon's operations with Schedly. From resource management to personalized services, we handle the complexities, so you can focus on delivering exceptional experiences.

How Schedly Helps

Service-Specific Scheduling

Define durations and assign services to qualified staff to prevent errors.

Staff Availability

Manage individual schedules with real-time updates for emergencies.

Resource Allocation

Book treatment rooms and equipment to avoid conflicts.

Recurring Appointments

Allow clients to book regular appointments with their preferred staff.

Automated Notifications

Send reminders to reduce no-shows and enhance engagement.

Client Preferences

Store client details and preferences for a personalized experience.
